Who I am

Between 2007 and 2012, I studied midwifery in France and received my certification. This education focused on both normal and high risk pregnancy, pediatrics and preventive gynecology. Actual patient practice was a huge part of my education. As a student, I delivered 101 babies, including twins, triplets, breeches and premature babies. I experienced a lot of intense moments that are forever imprinted in my memory!

During my schooling, I also worked as a child care assistant in two different Parisian maternities. It gave me an even better knowledge of breastfeeding and postnatal care.

Then, I worked for a year as a midwife in Saint-Maurice and Lariboisière maternities in Paris (labor room and postnatal care) where I delivered 78 babies the natural way, and helped a few others during the cesarean section.

As I developed my pre/post natal skills I was able to fulfill my dream of opening my own office near Paris. During three years, my expertise included: insuring prenatal consultations, birth preparation with relaxation, consultations at home to monitor high risk pregnancies and help parents with their newborn after they came back from hospital. I also taught Ayurvedic baby massage and practiced preventive gynecology, including contraception, and pelvic rehabilitation.

A few numbers about my practice as an independent midwife :

  • 1085 pregnancy consultations and monitorings
  • 900 birth preparation courses
  • 900 post natal consultations
  • 4500 pelvic rehab sessions
  • 45 baby massage workshops
  • 500 gynecologic consultations
  • 2 homebirths

During these three years, I also received a diploma in pain management in the pre and postnatal period, as well as full training in pelvic rehabilitation.

At the end of 2015, I moved to Florida with my husband to support his professional pursuits. These last two years were a great opportunity to learn English and Spanish while immersing myself in American culture. I met and worked with many interesting people from different areas: Organic Farmers, Herbalists, Doulas, Chefs, Nutritionists and Artists. I am multifaceted now and I am really grateful for the experience I was given to live in the States.

I am particularly excited to adapt my recent experiences into interacting with parents and babies. I look forward to supporting you in Brooklyn and connecting with my passion and my art of being a birth professional. My years of experience as a French midwife uniquely position me to be an incredible resource to you as Doula, both by providing technical knowledge and spiritual support.

What I do

As I am new to Brooklyn and the doula system in the US, I have not set a rate for what I propose but a suggested donation instead. I believe that people will be honest according to their own means and level of satisfaction with my services, valuing my time and devotion for them in a fair way. Whether you can give more or less than the suggestion is not the most important. To me, every woman deserves a trustworthy and non-judgmental help during her pregnancy.

If you choose me as your doula, you can custom tailor a program to your needs based on the following services.

Prenatal meeting at your place

90 to 120 minutes - suggested donation $75

At least one visit is mandatory if you want me to be present during your labor and birth. It is recommended to organize a birth preparation as well. When I am with you, we can use your time as you want. If you need to talk mostly, that is what we will do. If you need help to understand a special treatment or diet, or if you want to start making a birth plan, I’m here for you. I can also attend visits to your hospital, birth center or at your midwife’s office when you have a medical appointment.

Birth preparation courses

60 to 90 minutes - suggested donation $50

The themes can be anything and everything that you care or have questions about. A first meeting will help determine a schedule that suits you best. In France, we often organize seven sessions during which I talk about the following subjects:

  • Anatomy of pregnancy and the different situations of emergency
  • The uterus contraction / how to handle the pain during labor
  • Labor, mobility, postures / medical interventions as epidural, labor induction, C-section
  • Expulsive efforts and posture for birth
  • Pelvic floor and related questions before and after the birth
  • Breastfeeding
  • The newborn care and the new rhythm in the parents’ life

Relaxation and exercises can be practiced during those lessons, customized according to your needs. The courses could be organized with other women / couples as desired, a good opportunity to hear the famous "stupid" questions which are usually beneficial for everyone!

Labor and birth support

Suggested donation $300

At home, in your birth center or in your hospital, whether you will have a natural or a medical birth, I can accompany you through that beautiful experience.

Post natal and baby feeding support

90 to 120 minutes - suggested donation $75

Identify your baby’s signs, adopt the right posture to feed your baby, tame this new rhythm of life, get support through the different difficulties you can face as a new parent, introduce yourself to perineal exercises... Sometimes, it is just about having someone to talk to or having time to take a shower and a meal!

Ayurvedic baby massage workshop

2 x 60 minutes - suggested donation $75

Learn how to give your baby a nice massage that will help him understand his body limits, digest with ease and have appetite, sleep well, and have soft skin. A pure moment of complicity once both parents and baby tamed it.
